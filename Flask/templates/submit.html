FAQ

What happens after you submit your x-ray?
Upon submission of your x-ray our model begins to analyze the image for any abnormalities. Upon review the output will advise if any abnormalities were found.

We leveraged a National Institute of Health chest x-ray dataset. The data set size is approximately 40GB in size and includes more than 112,000 different x-ray images.
